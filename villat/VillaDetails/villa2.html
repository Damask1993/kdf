Mountain villa OverView
Bedrooms:

The villas feature spacious, elegantly appointed bedrooms with plush king-sized beds and premium linens. Large windows provide breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and forests, offering a serene and tranquil atmosphere. Each bedroom is equipped with ample storage space, soft lighting, and cozy decor to make you feel right at home

Kitchen:

Fully equipped for all your culinary needs, the kitchen is a chef’s dream. With modern appliances, including a refrigerator, oven, microwave, and stove, you can easily prepare your favorite meals. The kitchen also includes all the necessary cookware, utensils, and a coffee maker to start your mornings right. Enjoy meals in the dining area or take them outdoors to the private terrace for an unforgettable dining experience.

Bathrooms:

Relax and unwind in the elegantly designed bathrooms, featuring high-end fixtures, a rainfall shower, and luxurious bath products. Some villas include a freestanding soaking tub, perfect for a long, relaxing soak while enjoying panoramic mountain views. Plenty of soft towels and robes are provided for your comfort.

Living Room:

The spacious living room is the heart of the villa, with comfortable seating around a cozy fireplace, perfect for those chilly mountain evenings. Large windows flood the space with natural light, and the open-plan layout ensures the living area is perfect for both relaxation and entertaining. For your entertainment, there’s a flat-screen TV, a sound system, and a collection of books and board games.

Outdoor Areas:

Step outside to your private terrace or deck, where you can enjoy the fresh mountain air, take in the scenic views, or relax in the hot tub under the stars. Many villas feature an outdoor dining area, BBQ facilities, and a fire pit, making it the perfect setting for evening gatherings with family and friends.
